How Dumpster Rental Works

Get your dumpster delivered in 4 simple steps. Most customers are set up in under 10 minutes.

1

Choose Your Size

Select the dumpster size that fits your project needs. Not sure? Call us for expert recommendations.

2

Schedule Delivery

Pick your delivery date and location. We offer same-day delivery for orders placed before noon.

3

Fill Your Dumpster

Load your debris at your own pace during your rental period. Keep it for up to 10 days included.

4

We Pick It Up

Call or text when you're ready. We'll pick up your dumpster and handle all disposal responsibly.

What Can Go in Your Dumpster?

Accepted Materials

  • Construction debris (wood, drywall, metal)
  • Household junk and furniture
  • Roofing materials and shingles
  • Flooring, carpet, and tile
  • Yard waste and landscaping debris
  • Appliances (additional fee may apply)

Prohibited Items

  • Hazardous materials and chemicals
  • Paint, oil, and automotive fluids
  • Batteries and electronics
  • Asbestos and contaminated materials
  • Tires and propane tanks
  • Medical waste

Overweight Fee

12.5¢ per pound

Charged only when the included weight limit is exceeded. No hidden fees!

Before You Rent

  • Do not overfill above the rim.
  • Distribute heavy materials evenly.
  • Keep prohibited items out of the dumpster.
  • Ensure clear access for delivery and pickup.
  • Schedule pickup before your rental ends if needed.
