The dedicated local team keeping our communities clean.

Connor

Founder

Originally from Newburyport, MA, Connor has a passion for serving people. After heading out to San Francisco State for college, he realized his heart was back home in Massachusetts. He returned east and founded Waste Universe to build something meaningful in his own community.

Peter

Logistics

Peter joined Waste Universe right out of high school in Florida. He keeps the day-to-day operations running smoothly, making sure every truck is where it needs to be and every pickup happens on schedule.

Damien

Head of Customer Experience

Mike
