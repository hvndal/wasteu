Meet the Team

Welcome to the Waste Universe family! From our family to yours, we're dedicated to keeping our neighborhoods clean. Get to know the dedicated local folks making it happen every single day.

Leadership & Office Team

Connor

Founder

Originally from Newburyport, Connor grew up with a deep appreciation for community roots. After spending some time out at San Francisco State, the call of his home state brought him right back to Massachusetts. He founded Waste Universe to cultivate something honest, dependable, and meaningful for his neighbors.

Peter

Logistics

Joining us fresh out of high school in Florida, Peter brings a whole lot of sunshine and hard work to the crew. He keeps our day-to-day operations rolling smooth as a country road, making sure every truck gets where it needs to be with a smile.

Erik

Regional Director

Erik is our down-to-earth route wizard, working hand-in-hand with Peter. He treats every dispatch like he's looking out for his own neighborhood. When he's not keeping the routes running perfectly, you'll probably find him hanging out with his dogs or 🏎️ tinkering with Corvettes in the garage!

Damien

Head of Customer Experience

Originally from the UK, Damien traveled across the pond to pursue religious studies. He brings a wonderfully grounded, folksy warmth to everyone he meets, ensuring every single person in our community feels heard, valued, and well taken care of.

Mike

Customer Service

With ten solid years of service under his belt, Mike brings a neighborly hospitality to every phone call. He treats every customer like they're family dropping by for a chat, making sure no problem goes unsolved and everyone walks away happy.

EV

Evan

Sales & Partnerships
